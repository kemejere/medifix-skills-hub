About Medifix Skills Hub

A leading healthcare training and consultancy organisation committed to transforming clinical practice across Nigeria and West Africa.

Who We Are

Building a Healthier Nigeria Through Knowledge

Medifix Skills Hub Limited is a premier healthcare training and consultancy firm based in Abuja, Nigeria. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to bridging the skills gap in Nigeria's healthcare sector by delivering internationally benchmarked training programs, strategic consultancy, and quality medical solutions.

Our team of experienced clinicians, educators, and healthcare management experts works tirelessly to ensure every healthcare professional we train is equipped to deliver safe, effective, and compassionate care.

  • Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Nigeria
  • Aligned with WHO, AHA, ILCOR, and international training standards
  • Serving public and private healthcare institutions nationwide
  • Committed to affordable, accessible, high-quality training
