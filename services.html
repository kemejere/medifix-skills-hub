Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions designed to strengthen clinical capacity, improve systems, and support better patient outcomes.

Service 01

Clinical Skills Training

Our flagship service delivers simulation-based, hands-on clinical training programs that meet international standards. We train healthcare professionals to respond confidently and competently in critical situations.

Programs range from foundational BLS courses to advanced ACLS, PALS, PHARSO, and custom institutional programs — all delivered by AHA/ERC-credentialed instructors.

  • BLS, ACLS, PALS, and PHARSO certifications
  • Infection Prevention & Control (IPC) workshops
  • Emergency Obstetric Care (EmOC) training
  • Custom on-site training for hospitals and clinics
  • Simulation lab access with high-fidelity manikins
  • Internationally recognised completion certificates

Service 02

Healthcare Consultancy

Our consultancy practice helps healthcare organisations diagnose inefficiencies, design better systems, and achieve their quality and compliance goals. We work with hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and government health agencies.

Whether you need help preparing for accreditation, developing clinical protocols, or improving patient safety culture, our expert consultants provide tailored, actionable solutions.

  • Hospital quality improvement & patient safety programs
  • Accreditation readiness (JCI, NHIS, ISO)
  • Clinical governance framework development
  • Health facility assessment and gap analysis
  • Staff competency evaluation and development plans

Medical Equipment Supply

  • Clinical simulation and task trainers
  • Diagnostic and monitoring equipment
